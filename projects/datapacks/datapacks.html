Blockcraft Season 3 Datapack

Minecraft version: 1.19.x | Creator: Mules Gaming

The custom datapack used in the Minecraft lets play, Blockcraft! This datapack adds 5 spells to the game that can be found if various structures in the game. To cast the spells, hold a wand in your main hand and the spell you want to cast in your off hand. You can get the wand by tossing a stick on-to a enchanting table.
